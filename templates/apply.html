About the School

Greenfield Academy is a premier secondary institution located in the heart of Kampala, Uganda. Founded in 2001, the school has consistently produced top-performing students in national examinations and co-curricular activities.

Our mission is to provide a holistic education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, moral integrity, and leadership qualities in every student. We offer both O-Level and A-Level programmes with a focus on sciences and humanities.
